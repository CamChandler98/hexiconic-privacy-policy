Hexiconic Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Summary

Hexiconic is designed to respect your privacy. The app does not require an account, does not collect personal information, and can be played entirely offline. Limited data is stored locally on your device to save your progress. If you choose to watch a rewarded advertisement, the advertising provider may collect limited device information as described below.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Hexiconic does not ask you to create an account and does not request personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Information Stored on Your Device

Hexiconic stores game data locally on your device, including:

  • Puzzle progress and completion status
  • Gems earned and spent
  • Daily reward claim timestamp
  • App settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Advertising Data (Optional)

Hexiconic includes an optional rewarded advertisement feature. If you choose to watch an ad to earn gems, the advertising provider may collect limited information such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • App interaction information

This data is collected and processed by the advertising provider according to their own privacy policy. Hexiconic does not receive or store this information.

How We Use Information

  • To save and restore your game progress on your device
  • To provide optional rewarded advertisements

We do not use your information for tracking across apps or websites.

Data Sharing

Hexiconic does not sell or share personal information. Advertising providers may collect data as described above in order to show you a rewarded advertisement. This is governed by their own privacy policies.

Data Retention

All game data is stored locally on your device. You may delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

Children’s Privacy

Hexiconic is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • You may use Hexiconic without watching advertisements
  • You may delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL.
